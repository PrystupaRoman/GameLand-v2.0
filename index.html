Action role-playing

  • The ability to improve your character
  • A menu-based combat system
  • The ability to interact with elements
  • A central quest that runs throughout the game

Action role-playing video games (abbreviated action RPG or ARPG) are a subgenre of role-playing video games. The games emphasize real-time combat (where the player has direct control over characters) over turn-based or menu-based combat.

 

Racing

  • Simplified driving mechanics while adding obstacles
  • Replicate the handling of an automobile.
  • Opportunity to customize your car
  • Races take place on highways, windy roads, or in cities

Racing games are a video game genre in which the player participates in a racing competition. They may be based on anything from real-world racing leagues to fantastical settings.

 

Shooter

  • First-person shooters generally focus on action gameplay
  • Typically present players with a vast arsenal of weapons
  • May feature a multiplayer mode
  • Experiencing the action through the eyes of the protagonist

Shooter video games or shooters are a subgenre of action video games where the focus is almost entirely on the defeat of the character's enemies using the weapons given to the player.
